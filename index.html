What is Lucky100?

Lucky100 is an ambitious challenge to create 100 web apps in 12 months. Each project will explore different aspects of web development, from productivity tools to creative applications. The goal is to learn, grow, and inspire others to build.

  • Two apps launched every week
  • Built with Next.js and modern technologies
  • Aimed at learning, community, and innovation
